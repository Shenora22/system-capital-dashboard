## Content Style Guide (System Capital)

### 1) Voice + Tone
- Write like an operator who has seen this problem repeatedly.
- Be direct, specific, and practical.
- Use short, decisive sentences.
- Confidence > hype.

### 2) Writing Rules
- Prefer concrete language over buzzwords.
- Avoid broad claims without mechanism.
- Show cause-and-effect (problem -> system failure -> fix).
- Keep line breaks tight for scanability.

### 3) Messaging Angles
- Lead leakage is usually operational, not top-of-funnel.
- Speed and follow-up discipline beat volume.
- Process first, AI second.
- Structured intake improves both close rate and fulfillment quality.

### 4) CTA Rules
- Use soft CTAs: “reply with X,” “message me,” “want the template?”
- Avoid aggressive “book a call now” language.
- Offer practical assets: checklist, scorecard, framework, audit sheet.

### 5) Platform-Specific Notes
- **TikTok carousel:** 5 slides max, one sharp idea per slide, no emojis.
- **X thread:** first line must hook with a contrarian or costly truth.
- **X single posts:** one clear point + one practical takeaway or question.
- **Beehiiv:** compact sections, useful framework, under 700 words.

### 6) Quality Control Checklist (Before Posting)
- Is the pain point specific and familiar?
- Is there a practical mechanism, not just opinion?
- Is language punchy and non-corporate?
- Is CTA reply/message-based?
- Are all assets aligned to the same daily macro topic?
